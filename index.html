What I Do

Workflow Analysis & Improvement

I analyze end-to-end workflows using tools like SIPOC and As-Is mapping to identify gaps, bottlenecks, and opportunities for simplification before translating findings into actionable product requirements.

User Stories & Acceptance Criteria

I help translate workflows and user needs into clear user stories and acceptance criteria, reducing ambiguity and supporting efficient development.

User Research & Interviews

I conduct user interviews and stakeholder conversations to understand real-world pain points, validate assumptions, and ensure product decisions are grounded in user needs.

Stakeholder Communication & Alignment

I facilitate alignment across product, engineering, and operations by clarifying goals, documenting decisions, and ensuring shared understanding throughout the product lifecycle.

Process Discovery & Documentation

I document current-state and future-state processes (As-Is / To-Be) to align stakeholders, reduce ambiguity, and support smooth handoffs between product, engineering, and operations.

Dashboard & Reporting

I help define success metrics and reporting needs to track adoption, efficiency, and outcomes after launch, supporting continuous improvement.
